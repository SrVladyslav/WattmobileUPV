General description

A project based on continuous learning and putting into practice the theoretical concepts that we acquire in the University. Propelled by the bustling minds of some students of the Politechnic University of Valencia, Wattmobile UPV takes from its roots a decisive commitment with one of the most transcendental contemporary problems, the global warming produced by the incessant emission of polluting gases into the atmosphere.

That is why at Wattmobile UPV we bet on the conversion of combustion vehicles into electric vehicles, and it is our commitment to show that based on teamwork and involvement we can be able to give shape to a competitive prototype, capable of fighting at the highest level with emissions 0.
